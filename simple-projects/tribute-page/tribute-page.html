Lygia Fagundes Telles

Brazilian Writer and Novelist

Lygia Lygia Fagundes Telles is a Brazilian writer born April 19, 1923. She began publishing early in her youth and won Camões Prize, the highest literary award of the Portuguese language. Her works have received honors and awards from Brazil, Chile and France. She was elected as the third woman in the Brazilian Academy of Letters in 1985 and holds Chair 16.(wikipedia)
